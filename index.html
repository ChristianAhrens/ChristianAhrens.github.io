About

14 years of engineering experience — the last eight at d&b audiotechnik, a world-leading manufacturer of professional loudspeaker systems and spatial audio technology. Currently leading product design for a next-generation immersive audio creative software suite as Senior Product Designer UI/UX, bridging deep C++ engineering with user-centred product thinking in a cross-functional agile team.

Technical foundation in real-time C++ application development on JUCE and Qt, across macOS, Windows, iOS and Linux. Shipped production software for spatial audio routing, network-controlled audio systems, and interoperability layers spanning OCA/AES70, OSC, MIDI and AVDECC/MILAN — one of those private projects grew into a commercial d&b product.

As AI reshapes what software engineering means, the combination of hands-on technical depth with full-product ownership — from concept through UX design to shipped release — is a rare and increasingly valuable pairing. That is the capability I have been building throughout my career.
